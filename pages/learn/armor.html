Armor

There are three different types of Armor in Deathmatch Classic:

  • Green Armor
  • Yellow Armor
  • Red Armor

Armor can be found on the ground and is equipped by walking over it. Armor does two things: it reduces the damage you take overall, and provides you with armor "points", which are essentially more health.

Green Armor

A set of Green Armor provides 100 armor points and reduces all incoming damage by 30%.

Yellow Armor

A set of Yellow Armor provides 150 armor points and reduces all incoming damage by 60%.

Red Armor

A set of Red Armor provides 200 armor points and reduces all incoming damage by 80%.
