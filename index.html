Home Page

DMC TV is the #1 source for news, resources, and other content focused on our favorite boomer shooter. Our mission is to provide a space for those who enjoy this rather obscure title to be able to interact with other like minded players. The website is focused on pooling many things together; content from the days of old, tips and tricks, details about the game, fun facts, and more. We've always found the early days interesting; when clans were active, frag movies were a thing, etc. There is always new content we've found around old forums being added to this site, so be on the lookout!

We also have a Steam group and a Discord server which can be used to meet with others and schedule times to play. DMC TV was created in 2018 by cookiefortress. After discovering the game via purchasing the Valve Complete Pack on Steam, he decided to create a "hub" where the small amount of active players could talk with one another. Some rough drafts of a website were made, but in the end DMC TV became largely inactive in the years following its creation.

In 2024, this website was launched and thus DMC TV is actively maintained now by cookiefortress as well as maxresdefault, another long-time member and player of Deathmatch Classic.

Enjoy your stay, and never stop deathmatching!
